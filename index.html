Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sesli Kur'anı Kerim ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. You can use all features of the App without creating an account or providing any personal details.

B. Locally Stored Data

Certain data you create is stored exclusively on your device (Local Storage) and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Favorites: Verses you mark as favorites.
  • Notes: Personal reflections or notes you add to specific verses.

C. Automatically Collected Data

To improve app performance and provide advertisements, we use third-party services that may collect information such as:

  • Device Information (model, OS version).
  • Usage statistics (pages visited, duration of use).
  • Advertising IDs (Google Advertising ID) used by AdMob for personalized advertising.

2. Detailed Permissions Disclosure

The App requests specific permissions to function correctly. Here is exactly why we need them:

FOREGROUND_SERVICE & MEDIA_PLAYBACK

Required to allow the App to continue playing Quran recitations when the screen is turned off or when you switch to another application. This ensures an uninterrupted listening experience.

MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS

Allows the App to manage audio focus and volume levels, ensuring that the recitation pauses when you receive a phone call or when other media starts playing.

INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Necessary to stream high-quality audio files from our servers and to download/display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party providers for critical app functions. These providers have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app infrastructure and stability. View Policy
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements to support the App. AdMob may use device identifiers to show personalized ads. View Policy
  • Expo Framework: The App is built using the Expo SDK, which may collect basic diagnostics. View Policy

4. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, we do not retain your personal information. Your locally stored favorites and notes remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California)

We respect the privacy rights of users worldwide. Because we do not store personal data on our servers, our data processing is minimal. Users can manage their advertising preferences through their Google Account settings.
